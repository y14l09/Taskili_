Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At TASKILI, we strive to ensure that all users are fully satisfied with the services provided through our freelance marketplace. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for requesting a refund.

1. Overview

Payments made on TASKILI are generally held securely until the project milestone or the entire project is completed and approved by the client. We encourage clients to review the work thoroughly before approving release of funds. Refunds are granted under specific circumstances to ensure fairness for both clients and freelancers.

2. When Are You Eligible for a Refund?

You may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Non-Delivery: The freelancer fails to deliver the agreed-upon work by the deadline, and no extension was mutually agreed upon.
  • Work Not as Described: The delivered work significantly deviates from the project requirements, description, or the agreed-upon scope.
  • Cancellation: Both the client and the freelancer mutually agree to cancel the project before the work has been completed and funds have been released.
  • Unresponsive Freelancer: The freelancer does not respond or abandons the project for more than 7 consecutive days without notice.

3. When Are Refunds Not Issued?

Refunds will not be issued in the following scenarios:

  • The funds have already been released to the freelancer after the client's explicit approval.
  • The client simply changes their mind after the freelancer has started or completed the work.
  • The dispute is filed after 14 days from the delivery date of the work.

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are entitled to a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact the freelancer directly through our platform to attempt to resolve the issue mutually.
  • If no resolution is reached, submit a dispute request to our support team through the project dashboard.
  • Provide clear evidence to support your claim, such as project requirements, communication logs, and the delivered files.

Our Dispute Resolution Team will review your case and may ask for additional information from both parties. A final decision is typically made within 7-10 business days.

5. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds are processed back to the original method of payment. Please allow 5 to 10 business days for the funds to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
